Competition Rules

Scoring

When the competition starts, you will have {{allottedTime}} to complete as many problems as you can.

Participants are ranked by the most problems solved. Two participants who solve the same number of problems are ranked first by least total time.

The total time is the sum of the time consumed for each problem solved. The time consumed for a problem is the time elapsed from the beginning of the competition to the submittal of the first correct submission, plus any necessary penalties (see below). There is no time consumed for an unsolved problem.

Penalties

Some problems may incur a time penalty, meaning that each incorrect submission will decrease the worth of a correct submission by adding the designated penalty to the total time for that problem.

Rules are based off of the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest.
